# SEO Plan - Flight Desk International

Flight Desk International implements a comprehensive on-page and technical SEO layer to capture qualified, high-intent travel traffic on North America/Australia to India corridors.

## Technical SEO Features

### 1. Title & Meta Management
- Custom page titles and meta descriptions generated dynamically based on the active sub-view or route page.
- Clear, keyword-optimized titles (e.g. `Flight Deals from Canada to India | Flight Desk International`).

### 2. Structured Data (JSON-LD Schemas)
Each sub-view injects rich structured markup into the document head:
- **TravelAgency Schema**: Defines office locations, verified toll-free line, working hours, and logo.
- **FAQPage Schema**: Formats list of FAQs into rich accordions directly on Search Engine Result Pages (SERPs).
- **Product / Offer Schema**: Embeds sample route fares for popular origin-destination pairs.

### 3. Clean Route Routing
- Simulated custom URLs/hash tags to provide canonical mapping.
- Semantic HTML tags: `<h1>` reserved for main page keywords, with hierarchical `<h2>` and `<h3>` tags.
- Optimized Image Alt attributes on all airline and destination photography.
